WELCOME TO JAPANESE AUTO REPAIR!

Japanese Auto Repair Specialist has been proudly serving the residents of Farmers Branch, TX, for over 20 years. Our auto repair shop is accredited by the Better Business Bureau, and we provide hassle-free scheduling options and full warranties on all of our work. Our extensive stock of high-quality products is priced at competitive rates, and our team has the experience to help you find a cost-effective solution to your problem.

Customer convenience is our primary concern, and we offer both local pickup and delivery and 24/7 towing services. Our locally owned and operated shop is also fully licensed and insured, and we'll always provide you with a free estimate so you'll know exactly what you can expect.

We offer senior, veteran, and AAA discounts. Rely on us when you're in need of:

  • Branded transmission service or repairs
  • Fuel system service or transmission flushes
  • Engine repair or check engine light diagnosis
  • Cooling system service
